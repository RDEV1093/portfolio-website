My Story

Hello, my name is Roslyn, and I am a UI designer based in Pennsylvania. Helping people, improving their lives, and being creative are passions of mine. I am dedicated to creating designs and products that go beyond creating something generic but serve to fill a gap and meet a need that is not already being met yet, all while being accessible so that everyone feels included and can enjoy them. My extensive background and experience in healthcare-related fields give me a unique insight into how to meet client's needs better and create better user experiences.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, traveling, going on walks, learning new recipes and trying new food, performing dance choreographies with friends, and doing arts and crafts.
